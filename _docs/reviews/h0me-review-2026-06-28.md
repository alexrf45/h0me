## Executive Summary

Headline: **a signature WAF now blocks in front of freshrss, homer, and gatus.**
Standalone Coraza (OWASP CRS 4.25.0, caddy variant) reverse proxies were
introduced per-app, in-namespace — `Gateway → HTTPRoute → <app>-waf:8080 → app` —
each with a starter Cilium L7 method-allowlist guarding the app pod. All three
run in **blocking (`On`)**, verified: benign → 200/302, SQLi → **403**, and a
disallowed `DELETE` Cilium-dropped (Hubble `reason: L7 policy`). **authentik** is
intentionally excluded (IdP; CRS false-positive risk); the **public tunnel
endpoints** (gatus-public, kromgo) are left to Cloudflare WAF.

Getting there cost four follow-up fixes after the cluster's Kyverno
`add-default-securitycontext` mutation hardened the container: writable emptyDirs
under `readOnlyRootFilesystem`, the caddy `NET_BIND_SERVICE`/`no_new_privs`
file-cap gotcha, and a seed initContainer for the baked CRS config. Captured in
`_docs/post-mortems/2026-06-28-coraza-waf-readonly-rootfs-crashloop.md`.

Everything else is unchanged from 06-24: platform green, no breakage. Carried
design/resilience work (M3 descheduler, N6 Cilium pin, A1 Proxmox automation, O2
kromgo badge, CI1/CI2) is untouched.

Recommended next sprint: **M3** (residual CSI restart counts from the 06-27 storm
confirm it's still worth automating) and **A1**; W1's internal-app WAF rollout is
done — only the authentik decision and the Cloudflare-WAF public-surface follow-on
remain.
