## Executive Summary

Headline: **a signature WAF now sits in front of FreshRSS.** A standalone Coraza
(OWASP CRS 4.25.0, caddy variant) reverse proxy was introduced in the freshrss
namespace — `Gateway → HTTPRoute → freshrss-waf:8080 → freshrss:80` — plus a
starter Cilium L7 method-allowlist policy guarding the app pod. The WAF is
**live, healthy, and detecting** (verified: SQLi/XSS probes trip CRS rules
942xxx/941xxx and `949110` anomaly score 23) but runs in **DetectionOnly** —
not yet blocking, and not yet on the other apps.

Getting there cost four follow-up fixes after the cluster's Kyverno
`add-default-securitycontext` mutation hardened the container: writable emptyDirs
under `readOnlyRootFilesystem`, the caddy `NET_BIND_SERVICE`/`no_new_privs`
file-cap gotcha, and a seed initContainer for the baked CRS config. Captured in
`_docs/post-mortems/2026-06-28-coraza-waf-readonly-rootfs-crashloop.md`.

Everything else is unchanged from 06-24: platform green, no breakage. Carried
design/resilience work (M3 descheduler, N6 Cilium pin, A1 Proxmox automation, O2
kromgo badge, CI1/CI2) is untouched.

Recommended next sprint: **W1** — functionally verify the L7 method block, tune
out false positives, flip Coraza to blocking, then generalize the pattern to the
other apps; in parallel pick up **M3** (residual CSI restart counts from the
06-27 storm confirm it's still worth automating).
